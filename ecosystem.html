TOUR MULTIECOSYSTEMS OF COLOMBIA

Description

In Colombia there are more than 1937 species of birds. 79 have been cataloged as endemic. 193 as semi-endemic including 5 species for the isles (Source Institute of research of Biological Resources Alexander von Humbolt, 2018); besides 623 species of amphibians, 467 species of mammals, 3274 species of day lepidoptera, 518 species of reptiles and more than 4270 species of orchids resident in Colombia.

Our great biodiversity is not difficult to understand considering its unique geography: Tropical Forests, Mountains In Los Andes, Valleys Interandean, Paramos,Savannah wetlands, Foggy Forests, Tropical Glacial, vast plains, Costs in two oceans, tropical jungles (Amazonas, Choco), Choral Isles, Mangroves, Deserts and two mountainous configurations in the middle of plains, the Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta, in the shore of the Atlantic Sea and the Sierra de la Macarena, located in the middle of the Eastern Plains. Everything said before, makes the Colombian Touristic destiny to be one of great interest, when you travel and learn about its ecosystems, thermal floors, its culture, its ancestral traditions, and its social development. At the same time this make a vast dimension of expectations, in front of natural landscaping and endemism usual in this tropical country.

This rout designed to know a path of rich cultural, social and environmental tradition, with the participation of the peasant communities of the base, structuring this way a responsible tourism. The rout starts in the high paramo mountains, to zones at 1200 msnm, visiting privileged and unique ecosystems in the two different mountain systems (central and eastern), creating a sense of appropriation in the different ecosystems present in our country.

Level of difficulty

This route has a level of difficulty contemplated from 1 to 3 in the scale of 1 to 6 (Source, guide of good practices about nature tourism).

What we are going to find

This program is developed from 1200 msnm to 4500 msnm, with temperatures that oscillate from 5°C to 26°C. This represents an altitudinal gradient very spacious and outlines a great biodiversity of flora and fauna, which will show part of its wealth in birds. The proposed rout counts with a potential list of 300 species. Of them an elevated number is found in a state of threat. Besides there is an estimated of 12 endemic species.
